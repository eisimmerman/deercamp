Tradition Builder Notes

CampTraditions should feel like the heartbeat of the camp: the recurring meals, rituals, remembrance, storytelling, and shared identity that makes one camp different from another.

Keep the titles short and the descriptions meaningful One strong sentence usually reads better on the public page than a paragraph.
Use group labels to create shape Opening Camp, Camp Rituals, and Legacy & Meaning help the public page feel organized instead of random.
Optional links are supported If you have a related article, memorial page, or reference, add a URL and the live camp page will open it from the tradition card.
Order matters The traditions will display in the same order you set here, so put the strongest or most defining rituals first.
