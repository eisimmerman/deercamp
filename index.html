What is a Camp?

A small private group — family, friends, or a shared tradition.

A Camp is a private space for a small group to collect memories over time. Think: one place for the moments you’d tell around a fire — not a public timeline.

  • Small groups: meant for people who know each other.
  • Simple posts: text, photos, and voice notes.
  • Continuity: memories stay organized and easy to return to.

Privacy & Trust

Summary here; full policy lives on the privacy subdomain.

DeerCamp is designed to feel calm and private. No public feeds. No engagement metrics. No pressure to perform.

  • Private by default: nothing is public unless you choose.
  • No ads: no tracking for advertising purposes.
  • Clarity: simple settings, clear labels, quiet UI.
