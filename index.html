What is a Camp?

A camp is a small, private group — family, friends, or hunting buddies — where memories are shared over time without feeds, followers, or noise.

Privacy & Trust

DeerCamp is private by default. Nothing is public unless you choose. There are no ads, no algorithms, and no engagement metrics.
