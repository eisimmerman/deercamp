What is a Camp?

A simple idea for small groups.

A Camp is a private space for a small group — family, friends, or a shared tradition — to collect memories over time. Think: one place for the moments you’d tell around a fire, not a public timeline.

  • Small groups: meant for people who know each other.
  • Simple posts: text, photos, and voice notes.
  • Continuity: memories stay organized and easy to return to later.

Privacy & Trust
