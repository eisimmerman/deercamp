DeerCamp is built for privacy. Below are the policies that explain how we handle data and how we protect it.

Security Policy

We design DeerCamp to reduce risk by default: limited collection, secure storage practices, and no public sharing layer. We continuously review dependencies and access controls.

Data Policy

Your memories are yours. DeerCamp focuses on storing what you create (text, photos, audio) and keeping it private. We do not sell personal data.
