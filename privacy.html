Privacy & Trust

DeerCamp is designed to feel calm and private — a place for small groups to save meaningful moments without noise, ads, or pressure to perform.

Principles

  • Private by default — nothing is public unless you choose.
  • No public feed incentives — no engagement metrics or algorithmic timeline.
  • Clarity over complexity — simple settings, clear labels, quiet UI.

What we collect (early launch)

  • Basic app usage needed to operate the service (e.g., account/session data).
  • Content you choose to store: text, photos, and voice notes.

What we don’t do

  • No selling personal data.
  • No ad targeting based on your content.
  • No public profile discovery by default.
