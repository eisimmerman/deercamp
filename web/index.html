What is a Camp?

A simple idea for small groups.

A Camp is a private space shared by people who care about the same moments — family trips, seasons of life, shared experiences.

There are no followers, no likes, and no algorithms shaping what matters.

Camps are intentionally small, personal, and focused on memory — photos, voice, and context — not performance.

Think of it as a digital place that feels more like a journal than a feed.
