Abbotsford, WI

1997

Year 4

Saturday, November 15 - Pre-season Scouting 

I get up at 6:00 a.m. in Minneapolis to leave for Wisconsin to meet the guys for pre-season scouting and to put up my ladder stand.  It is about a two and one-half hour drive if all goes well.  I try a short-cut route to the school forest this time, going up D into Owen and then across some back-roads until I get to the school forest.  I drive by Duane's truck and know that he is still out bow hunting.  I drive past the logging road trail that goes to the old base camp and park my Toyota RAV4 on the road where I will walk in to put up my stand.

Driving over for the annual scouting trip is always fun.  Prior to the trip, I get to go through all my hunting equipment, taking note of what new stuff I will need and what things from the last season that will need repair.  The time spent in the field scouting is as much fun as the hunt itself.  We always take the time to relive the hunts from the past season and talk about the deer sign and deer that Duane and Dan have been seeing while bow hunting.

Since Dan and Duane are still bow hunting, I carry my stand into the woods and set it up based on where I recall my portable home-made stand was hung last year.  The woods are even thicker this year, now two years out since the clear cutting.  I am done in about one hour and walk back to the RAV4 and drive over to the meeting spot across from the school forest.  Dan and Duane arrive around 11 a.m. and Gene arrives between then and noon.

We drive back to the school forest and set up Gene's ladder stand back along the creek.  the brush is very thick now, so we question how easy it will be to see deer, but there are good trails and sign in the area so we go ahead and set up the stand.  In the afternoon, Gene and I go sight in our rifles while Dan and Duane go back to bow hunting.  I leave for Minneapolis.  One week until the opener! 

Saturday, November 22  – The Opener 

There is a light dusting of snow on the ground making it easier to see deer movement.
