Drummond - Elk Mound WI

1992 - p.7.

Year 3 Bayfield County

Nov. 22  - Day 2

It must have been there for some time as it the hide is ripped to shreds with some of the ribs exposed.  We think it may have been a coyote or wolf or bear kill.  We can't explain why it was under a foot of debris.  The hindquarters were missing.

We cross the creek and walk back to the shack.  The Packers are on TV and Allen soon arrives.  We watch the game through the two-minute warning and then go out for the afternoon hunt.  Gene and Eric see no deer and go back to the shack after dark.  

Nov. 23 - Day 3

Out late to the stand...ready by 7 a.m.  I think I hear deer all around me but cannot see anything.  We hunt until 10:30 a.m. and then I help Gene take down his ladder stand.  Allen does a one-man deer drive in from Deer Lick Lake road on the south side to try to push out deer our direction.  Some of the other hunters west of 18 mile creek get shooting but not Gene and I.  We pack up and break camp around 1 p.m.  I drive down D to get my tree stand off the swamp island.  

Gene and I arrive in Cornell about 5:30 p.m.  Char Melville is home so we unpack our gear.  After calling Phil Arneson in Elk Mound for directions, we catch up with Char.  Gene and I go to sleep about 10 p.m. and wake up at 4:30 a.m. the next day.  

 Nov. 24 - Day 4

We leave for Elk Mound about 5:30 a.m. and arrive around 6:45 a.m.  Phil is just finishing chores.  He shows us the two does his hunting party has taken so far.
