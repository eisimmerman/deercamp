Richfield, MN

1993

Missing My First Season

Well, Cathy gave birth to premature boy and girl twins on 10/11/93.  Gee, they missed my birthday on 10/10 by 3 hours!  Christopher Ross Simmerman and Claire Britton Simmerman were 2 lbs. 5 oz. and 2 lbs 3 oz. and 14.5 inches at birth.  They were born at 27 weeks and 3 days.

Claire spent a month and Christopher a month and one-half in the hospital before coming home.  Needless to say, we were thrilled with our new children but also scared at the same time as new parents of premature infants.  

Thankfully they have grown into healthy and happy children with few medical issues.  

Here they are as toddlers in the backyard of our first home in Richfield, MN...two great reasons to miss my first WI deer gun season since 1971.
