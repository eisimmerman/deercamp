Abbotsford, WI

1999 - p.6

Year 6 - New Land

Sunday November 21 - Day 2 

Gene and I decide to go back to the same area and walk in to our same stands again.  Duane and Dan said they would come by later in the morning to look where I shot to see if they could find any sign that I had hit the buck I shot at.  After looking around for and hour they came up empty and stopped.

We all leave for the school forest for lunch.  Duane cooks up some venison and we listen to the Packer's game. After lunch, Gene and I decide that we will go back to Colby due to being tired.  Besides, I can't see very well to shoot.  After skinning out Gene's buck,  I decided to go back to the twin cities since I could not see well out of my shooting eye.  I leave after supper and only get as far as Menomonee where I stay overnight in a hotel.  Leaving early the next day I go to see a doctor in urgent care for my eye.  He says a stick must have lacerated the cornea and gives me some drops and pain medication.

Dave shout a nice fork-horn buck sometime during the opening weekend, not sure if it was Saturday or Sunday.  

Oh well, a short and disappointing year, but memorable as well!
