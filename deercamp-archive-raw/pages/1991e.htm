Drummond Wisconsin

1991 -  p.6.

Year 2 Bayfield County

November 11/23-24 Last Weekend

Allen, Gene and Fred go out to hunt after 9 a.m.  Gene walks in behind the shack all the way to Eric's stand in waist-deep snow.  After sitting for a while he got up and walked about 50 yards North.  Stopping to look around for a minute, Gene decides to return to Eric's stand.  As he turns to leave he notices movement under a pine tree whose branches are pulled down under the weight of the snow.  A deer!  Gene throws his rifle up to his shoulder only to find snow in his scope.  He digs it out and fires off a shot and the deer goes down.  As it struggles to get up Gene fires off his last four shots.  

Upon hearing all the shooting, Allen comes over and helps Gene drag the doe through the waist-deep snow back the one-half mile to camp.  Final tally is five does and one buck for the season.
