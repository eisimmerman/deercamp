Monday 11/18 - Day 3

Allen slept in.  Gene, Paul and I got out a little later to our stands, sometime around 7 a.m.  I took my tree stand down at about 9 am after not seeing any deer.  I walked over to Gene's stand and found out that Paul had stopped by him on the way in to the shack.  Gene and I then took down his ladder stand and I drug it back to camp.  We all cleaned the shack and packed up our vehicles for the drive out.  We decide we will alternate vehicles with the two-wheel drives in between the 4x4's so if someone gets stuck they can get pulled out.  We drove up County D to Grandview and then South to the "pissing bear" tavern to register our deer.  Gene and Allen agree to come back up for the last weekend to hunt. 

November 11/23-24 Last Weekend

Fred joins Gene and Allen to hunt.  A big snow storm moves through and drops 15" in the area.
