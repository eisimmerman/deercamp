Elk Mound WI

1992 - p.9.

Shot Gun Country

The standers let it come to the mainland and then take it.

Nov. 25 - Day 5

Gene and I arrive at a new field to hunt at about 7:15 a.m.  Some hunters have been standing since 6 a.m.  We make four drives but push out zero deer.  I decide to end my hunt at 11 a.m. and take off for the twin cities.  The rest of the party makes a few more drives and then quits in the early afternoon.
