Near Abbotsford and Wausau

November 22 - The WI Opener

Gene again used his network to connect to a buddy who has some land that Gene could rifle hunt on.   It was a smaller parcel of land that Gene and Ben could set up stands on and get to very easy to hunt on.

On opening day, Ben had a very small doe come out to him but he decided not to shoot.  Gene, on the other hand, had two does come out to him which he shot and killed at very close range.  So Gene was able to either use up his hunter's choice and bonus tags or both his and Ben's bonus tags.  Oh well Ben, just wait for the  2004 season....there's always next year!
