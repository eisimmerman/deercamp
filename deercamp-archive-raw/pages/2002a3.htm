Abbotsford, WI

2002 - p9.

Gene and Ben Simmerman

Opening Day - Abbotsford, WI

Gene and Ben drove over to the school forest and met the hunting gang for the "end-of-opening-day" recap.  Duane Meyer asked Gene if he saw anything and he said yeah, got a couple of deer in the trunk!  Duane said "really?!"  Gene said he shot two bucks this morning as the gang all grabbed their flashlights and looked at the bucks in the back of the truck.  We all grabbed a beer in celebration back at Duane's truck as Gene told his story.  Joe said Gene was "greedy" and asked him if he shot them both with one  shot.  Gene said he wasn't that good of a shot and proceeded to talk about running into Don Meyer's son-in-law.  This later proved to be a "hunt-ending event" as Don Meyer took Gene's stand down off his land and gave it to Duane.  So, once again, Gene was back to the drawing board in finding a new stand to hunt from.  At least this one paid off!
