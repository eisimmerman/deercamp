Wisconsin Deer Season

November 16 - Pre -Season Scouting

Because of my left-shoulder arthritis, I am only able to get the stand up about 10 feet off the ground.  While this "feels" high enough (when I'm up the tree) as I look at it when down on the ground it does seem low.  However, I decide to leave it alone and go with it.  This decision will later cost me an easy shot at deer on the opener less than a week away.

We then head back to Lorin's stand and mark out his stand trail with bright-eyes tacks so that it can be found easily in the dark.  Once back at the vehicles, we talk about the 2002 hunts from last year and other hunting stories.  In early afternoon we break with Lorin heading back to New Glarus and me to the Twin Cities.  

November 20 - Eau Claire

Lorin and I stay at the Excel Inn and head out for a few beers at the local bars.  On Friday, 11/21 we head back out to Phil's farm and go back out to the woods to do a final check out on our stands.  Everything looks good so Lorin and I head back to the hotel.  We stay in for the night with pizza and a movie (Terminator 3).  We get all our gear and lunches packed for the opener on Saturday 11/22.

November 22 - The Opener
