Abbotsford, WI

2001 - p.13.

November 19 - Day Three

Well, we made several deer drives that afternoon but did not push out a single deer.  Gene and I quit about 3 p.m. to start skinning and cutting up my buck.  We finished about 7 p.m. and I returned to the Abby Inn in Abbotsford for the night, drank a few beers and watched the Vikings get beat on Monday night football!!  On Tuesday a.m. I woke up and drove back to Minneapolis.  

Rumor has it that Dave made a one-man drive through the woods by the school forest and kicked out a buck that Joe shot - a six pointer!
