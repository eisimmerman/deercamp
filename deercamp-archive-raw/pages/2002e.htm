Elk Mound, WI

2002 - p6.

Day Trip Hunting

November 24 - Day 2

The woods, brush and grass were thick along the river, so it was a good hiding place for deer.  Arnie and I would be the drivers.  After directing standers to their posts, we hiked over to the start of the drive.  I chose to go along the river and Arnie was closer to the edge of the field.  About three-quarters of the way through, we heard six shots from one of the standers.  Phil also kicked up another buck and got a couple of shots at it as it ran back into the drive.  With all the shooting and me being nearly at the end of the drive, I decided it was "safe" to answer the "call of nature".  So....I dropped my pants and started to take a leak when I heard Phil yell that another deer was coming back through the drive.  I looked up to see a doe running flat out from my left to my right.  I quickly threw my double-barrel 20-gauge shotgun up and fired off one slug and then had to remember that I had to pull the second trigger to shoot the second slug.  Neither one hit the mark so the doe was safe for another day.  When I got out to the field I found out that Phil's son Jamie got the six shots at a doe running across the field, but never touched it.

We all had a good laugh and after hanging up the deer in Phil's garage, I took my buck's rack and some venison jerky and drove back to the twin cities.
