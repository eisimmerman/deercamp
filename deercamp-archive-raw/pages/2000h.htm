By Monday, the size of the hunting party starts to thin out as some go back to work.  When all have finished lunch Duane decides to go up north a few miles away to hunt an area he knows holds a big buck.  Gene and Ben decide to return to Wausau and Lorin and I decide to go back to the hotel for a break before the afternoon hunt.  We decide we will go hunt where Gene and Ben have hunted since there is virtually no deer movement where we have been. 

Back at the hotel, I check my email at work (since I am out of the office all week) and find that an emergency has popped up that will require me to work the rest of the afternoon to get it under control.  We agree just to hang back and relax and then to get up later Tuesday a.m. to go take down our stands since Lorin has to get back to Madison .  I can then get home one day early and then see about hunting with a high-school friend of mine around Eau Claire , WI who has access to about 1500 acres of land for deer hunting.

November 21 – Day 4

Lorin and I get our stands down and get out of the woods around 10 a.m.   We break with Lorin going back to Madison and me to Minneapolis .  In at Minneapolis , I call my friend Arnie about hunting Wednesday with his party in Eau Claire .  He is happy to have me help them out, as it will only be himself and his two sons.  I tell them I will be at their farm about 7 a.m. the next day.
